How to Play

The aim of the game is to move down blocks from the top of the screen. You can rotate the blocks and move them either to the right or left. The objective is to get all blocks to fill all the empty space in a line at the bottom of the screen; when you do this, the blocks will vanish. You will be awarded 10 points for every cleared row. If the blocks reach the top of the screen however, it's game over.
